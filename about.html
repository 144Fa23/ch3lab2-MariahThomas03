About Us

Wildlife rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility located in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabiliatate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescue is a a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Assoiciation National Wildlife Rehabilitation Association

We help many animals, such as:

  • Raccons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Did you find a injured or orphaned animal? Contact Us to see if we can help.
